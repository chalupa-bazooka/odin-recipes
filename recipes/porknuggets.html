Pork Nuggets Recipe (Real)

Damn, it's so delicious! It also scares off local muslims so you'll have more nuggets to enjoy without sharing with anyone!

Ingredients:

  • Pork
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oven that can go really hot (like hell)

Steps

  1. Just buy a pack of frozen nuggets and that's it
  2. Alternatively, slice a piece of pork and put breadcrumbs around each slice
  3. Cool, now put it into an oven for 5 hours
  4. Damn, I said 5 hours? I meant 30 minutes
  5. Well, whatever. Eat them all now
